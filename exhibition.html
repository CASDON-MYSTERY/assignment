ORIGIN EXHIBITION ACCOMPLISHMENT

EXHIBITION

This exhibition serves to showcase creative talents through quality and interesting works by locals especially to be displayed to the public to better appreciate the art of handicrafts. In addition, this exhibition involves weaving craft entrepreneurs in Kampung Rampayan Laut, Kota Belud Sabah. The selection of this company is due to the textile weaving craft industry of Kota Belud district is one of the well-known weaving craft companies in the state of Sabah and a large number in peninsular Malaysia.

  • The fine weaving process will produce quality products.
  • The spread of weaving skills among the younger generation can affect the quality in the quantity of Dastar produced.
  • Strive to preserve and highlight the ideas and forms of sustainability and translate them into art forms.
  • Putting a new benchmark of professionalism in terms of art exhibition management.
  • Build a network between Kota Belud Sabah.
  • Looking for a high-potential layer to present creative, innovative and quality work.
  • Exhibit the level of content and the relationship between works and social and cultural ties through style and creativity through handicrafts.
  • Development of highly knowledgeable human capital.
  • Instilling a culture of creativity and innovation in all areas
  • Provide knowledge consolidation and exposure to the prospects of handicraft career.

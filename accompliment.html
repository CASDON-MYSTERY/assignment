ORIGIN EXHIBITION ACCOMPLISHMENT

ACCOMPLISHMENT

This exhibition can showcase Dastar weaving handicrafts that some people do not know about the existence of the product until its production has been recognized. In fact, the exhibition conducted aims to describe the processes of weaving production from raw materials that have not been treated until it becomes a product that is ready to be marketed. What we are trying to highlight in the exhibition is how the level of production of handicraft products is stated with the value required in motivating oneself until the product is fully ready. In addition, in any enterprise there must be problems that will allow entrepreneurs to learn from the constraints faced in further developing the existing industry.

Another accomplish from the exhibition which is:

  • Villagers involved in this exhibition both directly and indirectly will be able to highlight the talents that are in each of them that have been hidden for so long.
  • Feedback from the government is a form of indirect assessment that can be used as a guide to the villagers involved in this exhibition.
